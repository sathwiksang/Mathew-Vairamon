A Legacy of Legal Excellence

Dr. Mathew M. Vairamon is an accomplished Attorney at Law licensed in Louisiana, the Southern Federal Court in Houston, Texas, and the United States Tax Court. With a distinguished career spanning over 25 years across India and the United States, he brings a unique global perspective to his practice.

Currently serving as a Lecturer at Prairie View A&M University and maintaining an active legal practice in Houston, Dr. Vairamon combines academic excellence with practical expertise. His roles as a Harris County certified mediator, Texas notary public, and former Correction Officer demonstrate his comprehensive understanding of the justice system.

As Vice-Chair of the Stafford City Planning and Zoning Commission and with extensive involvement in community, cultural, and spiritual activities, Dr. Vairamon is deeply committed to serving both his clients and his community.
